There are four plans available for using the bike, but the fourth one is only available to the residents of the city, so we won’t cover it here.

Monthly

Access www.bicicletar.com.br (or the app) and click the option “Cadastre-se”

Click on Menu “Passe” > “Adquirir Passe” confirm the option “Passe Mensal” and then inform your credit card details.

Yearly

Access www.bicicletar.com.br (or the app) and click the option “Cadastre-se”

Click on Menu “Passe” > “Adquirir Passe” confirm the option “Passe Anual” and then inform your credit card details.

Daily (App only)

Look in the map of stations and go to any of them to get the bike

Buy the daily pass on the App

Use the acquired pass to release the bike
