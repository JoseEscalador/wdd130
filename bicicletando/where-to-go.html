Beira-Mar

Beira-Mar is a really well-known avenue that, goes along the shore. There are a lot of buildings, hotels, restaurants, sport courts and etc., that make up the complexity and beauty of this unique part of the city. There is also a famous flea market called “Feirinha da Beira-Mar”, which is held almost everyday at night. The view of the setting sun with the boats ashore and the people going about their lives in the sidewalk, be it working or just trying to have a good time, is absolutely mesmerizing yet relaxing at the same time.

Parque do Cocó

This is the largest natural park in urban area in the North and Northeast of Brazil and the fourth largest in Latin America. It spans across 15 districts and has millenary dunes at it’s borders. The name comes from the river that crosses it: “Rio Cocó”. It also has two options of boat trips, a shorter one, that takes around 20 minutes and a much lengthy one, that sails to the sea and back, which takes around 2 hours.
